Synopsis

An ultimate warrior, the one and only Alchemy Emperor, Ling Han, had died in his quest to achieve godhood.

Ten thousands years later, and he, with the “Invincible Heaven Scroll”, was reborn in the body of a young man with the same name.

From then on, winds and clouds moved, as he set himself against uncountable geniuses of this new era.
