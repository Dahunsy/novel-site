About us

Murky Realms is a fan edit and novel translation site. The reason for making this site is because a lot of fan translation sites use machine translations and it’s difficult to understand the books especially for people who are not native English speakers. So, if you’ll love to read light and web novels that have been curated and edited for your basic understanding and pleasure, please stay with us.

If you have any questions, suggestions or concerns for us, do contact us (link to contact us page) or email us at contactus@murkyrealms.com.

P.S -We do not claim any rights to the contents of these books as they are not written or translated by anyone on the team. We only do a superficial editing of the book to make it more acceptable to the English palate.

You are welcome to write us anytime you like. Feel free to suggest an idea or a business proposal. We are sure will write you back. It’s a never-ending program! Thank you all for your support
